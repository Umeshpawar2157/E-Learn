Our Placements

E-learning operates with a dedicated placement team, ensuring students receive daily job notifications.

To effectively engage with the job search community, students shouldz follow these steps:

1. Complete at least 50% of the course with an 80% attendance record.
2. Successfully clear skill tests or mid-tests.
3. Participate in mock interview sessions with HR and technical experts.
4. Submit your project.
5. Seek a review from your faculty.
6. Submit your updated CV.

By completing these steps, students enhance their chances of successful placement through MindScripts Tech's resources and support.
